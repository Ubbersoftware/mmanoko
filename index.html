About the home

Distinctive curves, an easy layout, and shade where it counts.

This circular home stands out from the moment you arrive — a clean green-tiled conical roof over warm cream walls, sheltered by a mature thorn-tree on its own walled plot. Inside, the rooms follow the gentle curve of the building, giving the living spaces an unusually open, calm feel.

The home has just been repainted throughout and is tiled from end to end. A central passage links the two bedrooms — the master fitted with built-in wardrobes — to the fitted kitchen, living room, family bathroom with a tub and a separate WC.

Outside, the plot is fully walled and secure, with a WUC water supply, an electric geyser for hot water, and plenty of room to come and go in the shade of the acacia.
